## 📝 Tone Tips

✅ **DO THIS:**
- Speak naturally, like you're explaining to a friend
- Pause occasionally for effect
- Show enthusiasm about the solution, not the tech
- Point at the screen when explaining features
- Be honest about what it does and doesn't do

❌ **AVOID:**
- Reading directly from a script (sounds robotic)
- Using jargon without explaining it
- Talking too fast
- Long pauses or "umms"

---

## ⏱️ Timing

- Opening: 30 sec
- Interface: 15 sec
- Behind-scenes: 20 sec
- Results explanation: 60 sec
- Second demo: 30 sec
- Why it matters: 30 sec
- Closing: 20 sec

**Total: ~3 minutes** ✅

---

## 🎯 Recording Tips

1. **Record in quiet space** (phone off)
2. **Look at camera, not screen** (when explaining concepts)
3. **Slow down a bit** - feels natural on video
4. **Let results load** - don't rush past them
5. **Do 2-3 takes** - you'll nail it by take 3
6. **No background music needed** - keep focus on demo
